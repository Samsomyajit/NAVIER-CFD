# RealPDEBench: fluid–structure interaction

## Goal
Forecast coupled wake and structural-response dynamics under real/simulated data mismatch.

## Recommended models
PIBERT, FourierFlow, FNO, DeepONet, UPT, Transolver, and a DMD baseline.

## Critical checks
- preserve the numerical/real split;
- report error separately for fluid and structural quantities;
- evaluate phase and dominant-frequency accuracy;
- include uncertainty or risk coverage for OOD forcing.
