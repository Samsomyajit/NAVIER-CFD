# 3D automotive aerodynamics

## Goal
Compare geometry-aware surrogates on realistic vehicle shapes and production-scale meshes.

## Candidate datasets
DrivAerNet++, DrivAerML, and ShapeNet-Car.

## Recommended models
GINO, DoMINO, Transolver, UPT, P3D, AeroTransformer, and graph baselines.

## Required reporting
Surface-only versus full-volume prediction, drag and pressure coefficients, mesh statistics, wall treatment, memory, throughput, and geometry-OOD performance.

## References
DrivAerNet++; DrivAerML; GINO; DoMINO; AeroTransformer.
